The First Path Experience

We invite you to experience Pushkar where the creation of the universe began, glitching reality into being. Pushkar doesn't do well with speed; it's a place best known slowly, deeply, without engines or hurry. In this unhurried space, cycle with ease through sacred landscapes, where every turn reveals why slow travel is the only way to truly understand a place.

Daily Itinerary

Our carefully planned journey ensures you experience Pushkar at its most authentic. Click on each item to see details.

+

Day 1: The First Path

We begin with a brief before our gentle ride to the universe's sole shrine of creation, the only active temple of its kind in existence. We'll wind through peaceful countryside paths and hills to meet Pushkar's most revered soul, the living guardian of these lands. Here, we'll spend unhurried hours exploring the tranquil grounds, wandering through the surrounding landscape, and absorbing the peaceful atmosphere before returning as dusk settles.

Distance: 30km.

+

Day 2: The Last Light

Today, we'll set out through golden desert landscapes, passing farms and traditional Rajasthani settlements. Our journey leads to a welcoming village where we'll savor an authentic lunch of regional specialties. After our meal, we'll relax and explore the timeless surroundings before beginning our return. As evening approaches, we'll ascend via ropeway to Savitri Mata Temple, where we'll enjoy the desert sunset.

Distance: 40km.

+

Day 3: The Secret Gate

Today, we'll ride through farmlands, nurseries and past rustic villages, where the rhythm of rural life unfolds around us. The trail at one point glitches to a different landscape, guiding us to a serene hillside sanctuary. After exploring the peaceful grounds, we'll take a short nature walk to a panoramic viewpoint, where the landscape stretches endlessly before us.

Distance: 40km.
