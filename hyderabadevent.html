Ramazan Special Night Ride 2025

Event Details

Location: Hyderabad, India

Duration: 1 Night

Date & Time: March 22, 2025 | 9:00 PM

Start Point: Telangana Secretariat

Finish Line: Durgam Cheruvu

About the Event

Experience an exciting night of cycling with the Ramazan Special Night Ride in Hyderabad. This unique event, organised by Happy Hyderabad and Hyderabad Cycling Revolution, offers cyclists an opportunity to explore the city's vibrant streets under the stars.

Riders will journey from Telangana Secretariat to Tolichowki, concluding at Durgam Cheruvu, covering a scenic and thrilling route. This event promotes fitness, eco-friendly commuting, and a strong cycling community.

Note: This is a self-supported ride. Participants should carry their own hydration and cash.
